Placement Policy

General Guidelines

The following guidelines govern the placement process at our college:

1. Eligibility Criteria

  • Students must maintain a minimum CGPA of 6.0 to be eligible for placements.
  • Students should not have any active backlogs.
  • Attendance should be at least 75% in all subjects.

2. Placement Categories

Companies are categorized as follows:

  • Mass Recruiters: Companies that hire in large numbers with salary packages up to 5 LPA.
  • Core Companies: Companies offering roles in core domains with salary packages between 5-10 LPA.
  • Dream Companies: Premium companies offering packages above 10 LPA.

3. Placement Rules

  • A student can receive a maximum of two offers.
  • Once placed in a mass recruiter, students can apply only to core and dream companies.
  • Once placed in a core company, students can apply only to dream companies.
  • Once placed in a dream company, students cannot apply to other companies.
  • Students with two offers cannot apply to any more companies.

4. Code of Conduct

  • Students must be punctual for all placement activities.
  • Professional attire is mandatory for interviews and pre-placement talks.
  • Students must carry their college ID cards and resumes for all placement activities.
  • Any misbehavior during the placement process may lead to debarment from future placement opportunities.

5. Off-Campus Placements

  • Students are encouraged to apply for off-campus opportunities.
  • All off-campus offers must be reported to the placement cell.
  • The same placement rules apply to off-campus offers as well.

Note: The placement cell reserves the right to modify these rules as per requirements.
